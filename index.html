My Favorite Characters

Here is a description of my favorite characters from Modern Family.

Claire Dunphy Gloria Delgado-Pritchett Jay Pritchett Alex Dunphy
Claire Dunphy is the mother of Haley, Alex, and Luke Dunphy. She is married to Phil Dunphy, and she is the daughter of Jay Pritchett. Gloria Delgado-Pritchett is the mother of Manny Delgado and is married to Jay Pritchett. Jay Pritchett is the husband of Gloria Delgado-Pritchett and is the step-father of Manny Delgado. He is the father of Manny Pritchett and Claire Dunphy. Alex Dunphy is the daughter of Clare and Phil Dunphy. She is the younger sister of Haley Dunphy, and the older sister of Luke Dunphy.
